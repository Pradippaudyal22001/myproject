Information

“Ghumphir” means Travel in Nepali. And we help to do that. We are a group of Travel Enthusiasts, Photographers and Adventurers. We love travelling in and around Nepal and share our Stories. We have travelled all around Nepal, some known and some very rarely known places but equally magical.

Ghumphir began as Travel website Planner among a group of Friends, who then decided why not share the beauty of nepal all around the world. Posting to social media wasn't enough, and Ghumphir was born. Ghumphir Facebook Page, which is getting popular in a very short span of time, and then, we start webpage!, we are all over the web now. This website is another attempt to share our Travel Stories , Photo and recomand places to Nature Lovers around the Globe..
